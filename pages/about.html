About Me

The story behind the lens - passion, vision, and artistry

My Story

A Passion For Capturing Moments

Welcome to my world of visual storytelling. I'm XBastille, a professional photographer based in San Francisco with over 10 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments.

My journey began with a simple film camera gifted by my grandfather when I was just twelve. That modest beginning sparked a lifelong obsession with freezing time and preserving memories through the lens.

Today, my work spans multiple disciplines from intimate portraits to expansive landscapes, always with an eye for emotion, light, and composition that tells a compelling story.

My Philosophy

The Art of Visual Storytelling

"Photography is the art of observation – it's about finding something interesting in an ordinary place. I've found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with how you see them."

My approach to photography is deeply rooted in authenticity and emotion. I believe that the most powerful images are those that reveal truth and evoke feeling. Whether I'm shooting a wedding, a portrait session, or a landscape, my goal is always the same: to create imagery that resonates on a human level and stands the test of time.

I see each project as a unique opportunity to tell a story – your story – with honesty, creativity and artistic vision. My style blends documentary observation with fine art sensibility, resulting in photographs that are both genuine and beautifully composed.
