Questions

Frequently Asked

Find answers to common questions about my photography services

How far in advance should I book your services?

For wedding photography, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to ensure availability. For portrait sessions and smaller events, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient, but more time is always better, especially during peak seasons.

What is your payment process?

A non-refundable retainer of 30% is required to secure your date, with the remaining balance due one week before the scheduled session. I accept credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal payments.

How long does it take to receive the final images?

You'll receive a few preview images within 3-5 days after your session. For portrait sessions, the full gallery is typically delivered within 2 weeks. For weddings and large events, delivery time is 4-6 weeks.

Do you travel for photography sessions?

Yes! I love to travel for photography. Local travel within 30 miles of San Francisco is included in the standard packages. For destinations beyond that, additional travel fees apply which will be outlined in your custom quote.

What should I wear for my photo session?

I provide a detailed preparation guide after booking, but generally I recommend wearing solid colors, avoiding busy patterns, and coordinating within your group without matching exactly. Most importantly, wear something that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
